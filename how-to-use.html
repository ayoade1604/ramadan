How to Create Your islamic greeting Card

1

Choose a Template

Start by selecting one of our beautiful card templates. We offer a variety of designs to match any personality or occasion.

Click on the template that best fits your vision for the perfect birthday card.

2

Customize Your Card

Once you've selected a template, you can personalize every aspect of your card:

  • Edit the text directly on the card preview
  • Change the background color or use an image
  • Adjust the text color and font style
  • Modify the font size for different elements
3

Save and Share

When you're happy with your design, click the "Save Card" button to generate a unique link to your creation.

Use the "Copy" button to easily share your card via email, social media, or messaging apps.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Card

  • Keep your message personal and heartfelt
  • Consider the recipient's favorite colors when customizing
  • Use different font sizes to create visual hierarchy
  • Preview your card on different devices before sharing
  • Save multiple versions if you can't decide on a design
